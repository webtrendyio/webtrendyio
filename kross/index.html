Normal

This is normal style, scrollbars are set to auto.
.horizontal-scroll

What does a horizontal scroll website look like?

Use it for posts, lists, cards, video or images.

Why should you even bother using this?

If your site has lots of contents, you can reduce the vertical scroll this way.

Is it UX related?

Sure, grouping items in such way enhances your UX.

Any other benefits?

Let user to choose he/she wants to go through this or not.

What can I include?

Literally everything, video, image, formated text, etc.

Are custom scrolls fully supported?

Unfortunately NO, FireFox, IE and some other browsers may not support it.

Can I change the CSS?

Of course you can, even the SCSS is available for faster development.

Are there any prerequisite?

No, this is a stand-alone CSS plugin.

Will there be any upcoming updates?

There may or may not, it depends on buyers requests.

Will you help me with integration?

Yes, this item is supported through comments.
