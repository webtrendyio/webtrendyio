About the Plugin

WP Sound Click | WordPress Audio Plugin is a powerful WordPress plugin that enables click/hover sounds for your website. With WP Sound Click | WordPress Audio Plugin you can define small sounds for your menu items, icons, images or whatever DOM elements that you want. These audio interactions will make your site more appealling, fun and user friendly. This sounds will also greatly help users with low vision or other disablities to better interact with your website.
