WP Click Effects

WP Click Effects is a WordPress plugin that creates SVG animations for your clicks. You can select which animation to run on mouse click and where to apply them. The plugin is packed with a set of different animations to use. Animations are based on SVG and CSS so it runs smoothly and fast and are scalable and remain crispy at any size, any device.

How it works?

01
Install the plugin

After downloading the plugin, go to your WordPress dashboard, install and activate the plugin.

02
Add click effects

Add as much as click effects as you want. You can choose where the click effects to appear.

03
View click effects on your site

Now go to front end of your website and refresh the page to see click effects.

Why use click effects?

Click effects enhances your website design aesthetic and interactivity. Click effects could also be used to convey a meaning for the action that is going to take place. Click effect is a new trend on motion graphics and also web design that makes your website enticing and joyful to use.

Where to use click effects?

You can add click effects on buttons, links, images or any other areas of your website. The plugin configurations allows you to enable click effect on elements with a class, id, tag name or attribute that you define.
